Gedidone is a web application that allows users to create and manage sticky notes, as well as interact with a virtual pet meadow. The application is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, providing a seamless experience for users to organize their thoughts and enjoy a virtual pet environment.

The idea came from a desire to create a low-friction life management tool targeted towards individuals with attention disorders.
